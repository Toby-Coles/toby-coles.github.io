Who am i?

My name is Toby Coles, i am currently a student at Staffordshire University (Stoke-on-trent) studying Computer Games Programming (Bsc) honour. I have a passion for computers and music, hopefully one day combining both in some form, possibly as a final year independant project. I am looking forward to the challenge of creating game engines and delving into the world of game development and onwards.
